Web Danielspage 6: Timeline of the Internet

Num Year Event Description/significance
1 1957 The First Idears computor time sharing is invented, allowing multiple people to access the same computor at the same time and share its resources.
2 1958 DARPANet DARPA, the Defence Advanced Tesearch Program Agency, in America, creates the ARPANet, a large scale computor network to accelerate knoledge transfer. Many networks like this are made all over the world. Packet switching is also invented.
3 1960 NLS (oNLineSystem) Created by Doug Engelbart which does hypertext browsing, email and so on. He also creates the first mouse to use with this system.
4 1969 ARPAnet he ARPANet is brought on line, and internet as we know it is born.
5 1971 Project Gutenburg This was the first attempt to put books online, for free. This was also the first attempt to use the internet not for simpily computing or scientific purposes, but to access content online
6 1972 Email as we know it Email can now be used through ARPANet to communicate between two different computors
7 1974 TCP/IP Transfer Controll Protocal is not actually made, but the idea is decided on. This was an attempt to have a standard protocall to all transfer files through the internet.
8 1975 Email The first Email client, although email already existed, this was the start of Email as we know it, including for the first time a ‘reply’ and ‘forward’ functoinality.
9 1977 The Moderm The PC Moderm, the device that allows for the internet as we know it is created so now PC hobbyists can access the internet as well
10 1978 Bulleten Board System The Bulleten Board System, this was the first attempt at communication over the internet with possible strangers. From this, stemmed most of the programms and apps we use to comminicate today
11 1978 Spam Spam is also created, this is a huge annoyance to many people, and one of the few downsides of the internet
12 1979 MUDs MUDs, or MultiUser Dungens, were entirely text based games where users navigated dungens. These games would spark huge games such as World Of Warcraft. These usually also included a chat feature.
13 1979 Usenet Usenet becomes a way for UNIX computors to comminicate. Though not officially part of the internet, it was still a big step forward in online chatting
14 1980 ENQUIRE CERN creates an early search engine for keeping track of people, software and projects
15 1981 BITNET BITNET connects educational IBM mainframes and allows for Emails between these computors
16 1982 Minitell An online chess game with a chat feature, this is one of the first online multyplayer games with a GUI.
17 1983 ARPANet switches protocalls ARPANET switches to TCP/IP, which is another step forward towards a completely standardised internet.
18 1988 Internet Atack The first internet atack, 'The Morris Worm' was the first internet based atack and messed with much of the interne
19 1989 AOL America Online, the first online messaging program to really take off, helped to popularise the internet.
20 1989 A Search Engine Archie, the first openly available internet search engine is created by McGill University and becomes so popular that they dissable outside access so as not to slow it down.
21 1990 WWW The World Wide Web is created and WWW protocalls, the standards for HTTP, HTTPS and URLs, are all completed further standardising the internet
22 1992 Mosaic The Mosaic browser is made, this has a GUI and was the first widely popular browser
23 1991 Gopher The first user frindly interface for web browsing is born, called Gopher, it did not require users have a great knoladge of UNIX, and features clickable links. VERONICA is also created to search for webistes on Gopher
23 1998 Internet Explorer Microsoft releases Micosoft Internet Explorer as part of its 'Windows 98' os. This is the final nail in the coffin of a purely scientific internet and opens the floodgates for comertial use.
24 1998 Google! Google, now by far the most popular search engine, first goes online
25 2000 Dotcom Colapse The dotcom collapse, hundreds of companies colapse, causing some people to loose some faith in the internet and discuraging further internet bassed buisneses.
26 2001 - 2006 Social Media First MySpace becomes popular, the Facebook launches and the term 'social media' is coined. Then in 2005 and 2006, Youtube and Twitter are made.
26 2007 The iphone The iphone, this not only revolutionised computing in general, but also the internet with the sparking the development of mobile web design and apps
